INTERMEDIATE

Signature Pasta Dish

Prep Time: 20 min Cook Time: 25 min Servings: 6

Description: A restaurant-quality pasta dish that combines traditional techniques with modern flavor profiles. Perfect for dinner parties or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fresh pasta (or high-quality dried)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • Fresh basil for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil for pasta
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  3. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute
  4. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes until slightly thickened
  5. Cook pasta according to package directions, reserve 1 cup pasta water
  6. Drain pasta and add to sauce, tossing to combine
  7. Add parmesan cheese and pasta water as needed for consistency
  8. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with fresh basil

Chef's Tips

  • Always reserve pasta water—the starch helps create a silky sauce
  • Use freshly grated parmesan for best flavor and texture
  • Can add protein (chicken, shrimp) or vegetables for variation
  • Leftovers can be refrigerated for up to 3 days

How to Add New Recipes

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Prepare Images: Add recipe photo to /images/recipes/ folder
  2. Copy Template: Copy the "recipe-card" div section above
  3. Set Difficulty: Choose EASY (green), INTERMEDIATE (orange), or ADVANCED (red)
  4. Add Recipe Details: Fill in title, times, servings, description
  5. Add Video: Replace VIDEO_ID_HERE with your YouTube video ID
  6. List Ingredients: Include all ingredients with measurements
  7. Write Instructions: Number steps clearly and include timing
  8. Add Tips: Include helpful chef's tips and variations
  9. Create PDF: Make a downloadable PDF version and place in /recipes/ folder

Difficulty Levels:

  • EASY: background: #27ae60; (green) - Basic techniques, minimal ingredients
  • INTERMEDIATE: background: #f39c12; (orange) - Some advanced techniques
