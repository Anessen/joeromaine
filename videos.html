How to Add New Videos

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Get Video ID: From your YouTube URL youtube.com/watch?v=ABC123, the ID is ABC123
  2. Copy Template: Copy the "video-entry" div section above
  3. Replace VIDEO_ID_HERE: Paste your video ID in the iframe src
  4. Update Category: Change category (ENGINEERING, COOKING, DIY, SECURITY, FITNESS)
  5. Add Title & Description: Fill in your video title and description
  6. Set Date: Add publication date

Category Colors:
